Introduction to Characters



Obito Uchiha

Obito Uchiha is a character from the anime and manga series Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto.


Eiji Sawakita

Eiji Sawakita is a fictional character from the basketball manga and anime series Slam Dunk,
created by Takehiko Inoue. He is the ace player of Sannoh Industry Affiliated High School,
widely regarded as the top high school basketball player in Japan.
